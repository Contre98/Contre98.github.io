Simple & Authentic. Redifining Luxury

Reconnect with nature in the remote Patagonian untamed vastness. A willing action in favor of conservation, with thousands of hectares dedicated to nature tourism.

Our private nature reserve is located 27 km from El Chaltén, on the scenic Provincial Route 41,in De las Vueltas river valley, province of Santa Cruz. We were born with the aim to safeguard the beauty of the forest, the fauna, the rivers, the lakes and the glaciars of this rural environment, harmoniously integrating investigation, education and tourism.
We create a new space, a warm and comfortable atmosphere where, together with our guests and scientific researchers, we share the generous deployment of nature.
